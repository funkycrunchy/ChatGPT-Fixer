# Privacy Policy – ChatGPT Fixer

ChatGPT Fixer does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

## What this extension does
- Improves performance of long ChatGPT conversations by limiting how much conversation history is loaded into your browser.
- All processing happens locally within your browser.

## Data collection
- No personal data is collected.
- No analytics, tracking, or telemetry are used.
- No data is sent to external servers.

## Storage
- The extension stores user preferences (such as history size settings) locally using Chrome’s storage APIs.
- This data never leaves your device.

## Third parties
- The extension does not communicate with any third-party services.

## Support channels
Support for ChatGPT Fixer is provided via the following channels:

- **GitHub** – for bug reports, issues, and code transparency.
- **Discord** – for community support and discussion:  
  https://discord.gg/V4PBZ9eyD3

If you choose to join the Discord server, any information you share there is voluntary and subject to **Discord’s own privacy policy and terms of service**. ChatGPT Fixer does not collect or process any data from Discord.

## Scope
The extension only runs on:
- https://chat.openai.com/*
- https://chatgpt.com/*

## Contact
For support, questions, or issues, please use the GitHub repository or the Discord server linked above.
